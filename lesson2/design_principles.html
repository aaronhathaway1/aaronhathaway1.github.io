Visual Hierarchy

JC Penney

https://m.jcpenney.com/

Here JC Penney draws attention to their 50% off sale by putting it next to a person and with larger text and a cta. Second, we notice their 60% and 40% deals. Finally, we see the rest of their page as normal.

Hick's Law

Tesla

https://www.tesla.com

Tesla utilizies Hick's Law. They simplified the user's initial choice by giving them the option of customizing an order or looking at initial inventory. Once the user is past this point and more involved/invested more options are presented. Contrast is also used effectively.

Contrast

Excalibur MGM Resorts

https://excalibur.mgmresorts.com/en.html

This casino and hotel website makes good use of contrast with the colors white and a reddish brown. The color difference is subtly used to bring balance to the page while also highlighting 2 cta buttons.
