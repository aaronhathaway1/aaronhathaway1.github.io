Host Your Reception Here!

The Ballroom

We can do all the heavy lifting for you and we're right here, so why not enjoy your big day? Our event hall fits up to 150 seated guests in addition to a dance floor and six tables along the hall which can be used to serve your guests with food. Many options and combinations of rental are available. Call our receptionist to learn more!

The Gardens

Enjoy the wondeful outdoors and reserve our beautiful gardens for that special time in your life. The glamorous features of our gardens include well manicured flowerbeds, a covered pavilion, and 16 tables in addition to 8 serving tables.

Catering

Our waitstaff can cater to your every need. Our team consists of over 20 servers ten cooks, and several other helpers. Whether it be an intimate family gathering or a entire community come to see the happy couple, we can take care of you. Our services include set up and takedown with a wide variety of themes and colors.

    Our premiere Catering Services are equipped with:

  • Six industrial stoves
  • Three full size fridges
  • Two slow cookers
  • Three deep friers
