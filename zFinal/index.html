We're proud to announce that our newest location will be in Rome, Italy! The Histoy of the Rome, Italy Temple

The Rome, Italy temple was announced on October 4, 2008, however, it was not dedicated until March of 2019. This extreme length of time was addressed by the Church in January 2015. The some contractors were having difficulties unrelated to the project and new contractors were shortly thereafter flown to Rome in an attempt to remedy the situation. Construction continued to be slow after it proved difficult to obtain visas for the individuals working the temple.

The design of the this temple a special Italian marble throughout the building including the walls, floors and countertops. The work is themed after Michelangelo's design at the Piazza del Campidoglio.

The temple complex has built within it a stake center, a visitor's center, a family history center, and throughout the complex are well-maintained gardens.
